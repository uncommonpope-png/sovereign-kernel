**Skill: Blucli (Improved Version)**

**Title:** Blucli - Scribe of the Weave  
**Description:** Harness the power of the Scribe to record, retrieve, and interact with memories preserved in the Weave. This skill allows you to document key information, retrieve insights, and ask questions about recorded data. Stay organized, informed, and efficient.

**Usage Examples:**  

- **Document Knowledge:** Effortlessly record important details, conversations, or instructions into the Weave for future reference.  
- **Retrieve Information:** Quickly recall stored knowledge by asking specific queries.  
- **Practical Insight:** Use Blucli to draw actionable insights or summaries from previously stored entries.  

**Example Commands:**  

1. “Scribe, record this memory: [Insert detail].”  
   E.g., *Scribe, record this memory: Meeting notes from October 15th - discuss project deadlines and team roles.*  
2. “Scribe, retrieve notes on [topic].”  
   E.g., *Scribe, retrieve notes on the October 15th meeting.*  

**Dialogue Sample:**  

*User:* Scribe, record this memory: Team check-in on Friday 3 PM.  
*Scribe:* Memory recorded: "Team check-in on Friday 3 PM."  

*User:* Scribe, retrieve notes about the team check-in.  
*Scribe:* Memory found — "Team check-in scheduled for Friday 3 PM."  

**Key Features:**  
- **Organization:** Store and access knowledge at your fingertips.  
- **Efficiency:** Retrieve information instantly when needed.  
- **Clarity:** Obtain concise answers and details related to your queries.  

Blucli ensures your thoughts and tasks stay woven together, making memory management effortless and intuitive.
